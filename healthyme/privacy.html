Privacy policy

Healthy Me is operated by Central Three LLC and is designed to keep your information private.

Information you provide

When you use Healthy Me, you may enter information such as your skin type, hair type, concerns, complexion details, routines, and reminder times. This information is stored only on your device. We do not operate a server that receives or stores it, and we cannot see it.

No account

Healthy Me does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, or contact information.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Healthy Me schedules notifications locally on your device. No notification data is sent to us or to any third party.

No data collection or sharing

Healthy Me does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information with anyone.

Affiliate links

Some product links in the app are affiliate links. If you tap one, you leave the app and visit the retailer's website in your browser; that retailer's own privacy policy applies from that point. We may earn a commission on qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships never change which products we recommend or how they are ranked.

Deleting your data

You can remove all stored information at any time using the Reset option in the app's Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children

Healthy Me is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time and will revise the date above when we do.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@centralthree.site.

This policy reflects the current version of Healthy Me, which stores all personal data on-device and transmits none. If a future version adds features that send data off the device (such as accounts, sync, analytics, or live product feeds), this policy will be updated first.
