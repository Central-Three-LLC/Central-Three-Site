How it works

Three steps to a routine you'll actually keep

No accounts, no feeds, no noise — just a clear plan and a nudge at the right moment.

🧭

Tell us about you

A short assessment of your skin type, hair type, and what you'd like to work on. Optionally add your complexion for makeup and hair-color suggestions.

🌿

Get your routine

Morning, evening, and weekly routines built from products matched to you — ranked by fit, always. You can check off each step as you go.

🔔

Make it a habit

Gentle reminders at the times you pick, streaks with grace days, and weekly summaries that encourage — never guilt.

Small makers

We spotlight small businesses

Healthy Me features independent and small-batch beauty brands alongside your matched products. Hand-picked, clearly labeled, and never allowed to change your match results — fit comes first, always.

Private by design

Your information stays on your device

Healthy Me works without an account. Your profile, routines, and progress are stored locally on your phone — we don't have a server that sees them.

  • No account required. Use the whole app without signing up.
  • Local-first storage. Your data lives on your device, not ours.
  • Reminders are local. Notifications are scheduled on your phone.
  • Delete anytime. Reset in Settings or uninstall — it's gone.
A note on wellness. Healthy Me offers general skincare and haircare suggestions to help you build a routine. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for a dermatologist or other qualified professional. If you have a skin or scalp condition, an allergy, or any concern about a product or ingredient, talk to a professional first.
